Phase 1 · MPCCL Draft System

Auction & Player Pathway

Clean, merit-based flow: trials → graded pool → franchise draft → ICPLT20 season.

How players enter the league
All players (corporate and club) enter through the same door: **open trials**. We grade every player on skills, fitness and temperament, then build a ranked pool from which franchises create squads.
No random entries
Transparent grading
Corporate + Club mix
Step 1
Trials
Players register online, attend structured trials, and are evaluated on batting, bowling, fielding and game awareness.
Step 2
Graded Pool
Selected players enter the ICPLT20 pool with a grade tag (for example: A, B, C) to help franchises balance their squads.
Step 3
Franchise Draft
8 MPCCL franchises build their squads from this pool, following the fixed corporate + club player rules for Phase 1.
Squad structure & flow Phase 1 MPCCL
High level view of how a season will be built. Detailed rules, dates and fees will be announced when registrations open.
Layer What it means Notes
Squad Size Each franchise maintains a fixed-size squad (corporate employees + local club players). Exact numbers and mix will be published before trials.
Corporate Players Employees registered by their organization or franchise owner. They form the core of each ICPLT20 team. Registration via corporate/individual owner; performance tracked across seasons.
Club Players Above-19 local cricketers selected through trials. Limited slots per team to keep the league corporate-first. League-supported match fee model for club players in official fixtures.
Draft / “Auction” Franchises pick from the graded pool within pre-defined squad rules (e.g. max club players per team, minimum corporate players). Point-based, not a noisy bidding war – designed to be simple and fair.
Season League matches into playoffs and a final. All fixtures use the same ICPLT20 ruleset for consistency season to season. Live scoring + basic video coverage planned from Season 1.
