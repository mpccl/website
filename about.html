About ICPLT20

Why ICPLT20 Exists

Corporate cricket in India has passion — but no structure. Players train, compete, and invest time… yet most tournaments feel temporary: no professional format, no long-term recognition, no player identity.

ICPLT20 changes that. We are building a **real cricket ecosystem** for working professionals and club-level talent — with trials, franchises, content, stats, and continuity.

The Format Vision

The league starts with **Phase 1: MPCCL – 8 corporate franchises** in Madhya Pradesh. Each team represents an organization or regional owner and follows a standardized squad model: corporate employees + selected club players.

As seasons progress, the league expands state-to-state, eventually creating:

**→ A national corporate championship with franchise legacy.**
